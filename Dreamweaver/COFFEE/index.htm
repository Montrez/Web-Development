Global Bean’s Mission

The Global Bean's mission is to educate coffee lovers about the coffees of the world.

“A morning without coffee is like sleep.”

Coffee tastes are a matter of individual preference. The differences in coffees are found in the country or region of original and the quality of grade. Coffees from different regions have distinct and delicate flavors. The flavor is also influenced by the harvest time, method of processing, and the degree of roast.

 

 

Tips for brewing that perfect cup:

  1. Use fresh, high quality coffee.
  2. Select the correct grind.
  3. Use the proper amount of coffee.
  4. Add pure water.
  5. Drink within half an hour of brewing.
  6. Store coffee grinds or beans in a cool, dark, airtight container.
