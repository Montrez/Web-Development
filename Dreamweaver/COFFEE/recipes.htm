Vanilla Iced Coffee

6 cups cold coffee
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup cream

Combine ingredients.

Add sugar to taste.

Serve in tall glasses with lots of ice.

Cappuccino

1/3 cup expresso
2/3 cup milk
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon or nutmeg
1 teaspoon chocolate or vanilla powder

Add the expresso to a large cup.

Steam and froth the milk.

Pour the steamed milk onto the expresso, holding back the froth with a spoon.

Add the froth.

Sprinkle toppings.

Cafe au Lait

2/3 cup of brewed coffee
1/3 cup milk
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 teaspoon chocolate powder

Steam the milk.

Pour the brewed coffee in a large cup.

Add the milk to the coffee to create a layer of foam on top.

Sprinkle the cinnamon and chocolate on the foam.
