Most operating systems have built-in performance and maintenance features. For example, Windows XP has a Performance and Maintenance category where the user can select options such as “Free up space on your hard disk” and “Rearrange items on your hard disk to make programs run faster.” It is also a good idea to delete any unnecessary files from the hard disk on a regular basis.
