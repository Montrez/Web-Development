Many companies that produce computer applications provide periodic updates for downloading at their company website. One important application to maintain is virus protection software. New viruses are continually being created, so new virus definitions must be downloaded on a regular basis to protect against the new viruses. Most virus protection programs have options where virus definitions can be updated automatically on a regular basis. Operating system software also requires periodic updating to protect the computer from security issues. Like virus protection software, operating system software has options where the updates can be downloaded automatically.
