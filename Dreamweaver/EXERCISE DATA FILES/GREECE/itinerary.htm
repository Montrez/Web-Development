Itinerary

Day 1

Depart Miami, Florida, U.S.A.

Arrive Athens, Greece.

Stay in Athens.

Day 2

Travel by hydrofoil to the island of Hydra.

Hike around Hydra.

Stay in Hydra.

Day 3

Travel by hydrofoil to Leonedion.

Have dinner at the plaka.

Stay in Leonidion.

Day 4

Drive to the Diros caves for the day.

Swim at Megalo Botsalo beach.

Day 5

Drive to Monemvasia.

Hike to the top.

Drive back to Leonidion.

Day 6

Drive to Kosmas.

Explore the town.

Drive back and stay in Leonidion.

Day 7

Travel by hydrofoil to Athens.

Fly back to Miami.
