I recently visited the beautiful country of Greece. This trip was my first visit to Greece and hopefully will not be my last visit! I went to the island of Hydra (pronounced "EE-druh") and traveled around an area known as the Peloponnese (pronounced "pell-lo-pone-EECE"). This site contains photographs I took as I traveled, and a chronological list of the places I visited.
