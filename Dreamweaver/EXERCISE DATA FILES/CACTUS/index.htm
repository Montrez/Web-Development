Cacti are fascinating plants. They flourish in dry, hot climates. How do they retain the moisture that other plants cannot? Cacti wait until the cooler hours in the night to open tiny pores that take in carbon dioxide, and therefore avoid losing moisture through the pores.

Contrary to popular myths, most cacti flower at least once a year. The flowers on cacti can be in a wide range of colors and flower sizes. Cacti can be found growing out of rocks and in the harshest of environments. This site explores the diversity of the cactus plant.
