SI Derived Units

Everything can be measured in terms of the seven SI base units: meter, kilogram, second, mole, kelvin, ampere, and candela.

SI derived units are combinations of SI base units. For example, the unit of area is a square meter. A square meter is formed from an SI base unit (meters x meters), but not one of the seven SI base units.
