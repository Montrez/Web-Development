Jupiter

Jupiter is the fifth planet from the sun at 778.3 million km and is the largest planet in our solar system. It has 16 known satellites. Jupiter has a rocky core about the size of Earth, which is surrounded by liquid hydrogen ocean. It takes 9.8 hours to rotate about its axis.
