Solar System
 

Introduction

Our solar system is made up of the nine planets and other objects orbiting the sun. The solar system is estimated to be about five million years old and there are many theories on how the solar system was formed. Stars are also part of our solar system.

Through observation and space exploration we have learned much about our solar system. However, there is a lot that we do not know. Advances in technology allow us to learn more about our solar system every day.
