Many careers require education beyond high school. Colleges and universities offer a diverse range of degree programs to help prepare students for a selected career. Selecting a college or university degree option is a big decision. There are many factors that need to be examined. This Web page contains many helpful hints.

Selecting a College or University

Before selecting a college or university be sure to:

  • research the school by going to their web site
  • take a tour of the school
  • talk to former graduates or current students
  • talk to an advisor at the school
