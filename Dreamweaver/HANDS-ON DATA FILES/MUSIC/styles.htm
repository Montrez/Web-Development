Styles of Music

There are so many different styles of music, such as jazz and rock. Music differs in the way it is composed and performed. Here is a list of music styles:

bluegrass
a type of country music that uses acoustic instruments

calypso
folk-style music from the Caribbean islands

classical music
a style of art music

contemporary
music that is current

folk music
uncomplicated music that speaks directly of everyday matters

jazz
a style of dance music

ragtime
a style of jazz music

rhythm and blues (R&B)
a style of popular music that combines blues harmonies and rhythm with gospel-like vocals

rock
a style of popular music that features guitars and a driving rhythm
