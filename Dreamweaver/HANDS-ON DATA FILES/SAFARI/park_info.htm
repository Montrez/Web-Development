Park Hours:
Monday - Friday 9 am to 5 pm
Saturday 8 am to 6 pm
Sunday 10 am to 6 pm

Directions from the south:
Take I-95 north to the Jog Exit. Go east 4 miles, we are on the right.

Directions from the nouth:
Take I-95 south to the Jog Exit. Go east 4 miles, we are on the right.

Time Frame
The drive through Safari Park is 12 miles long. The time spent on the drive will vary, with a family spending an average of about three hours at the park. You may drive your own car through the park or have a tour guide take you through in a safari bus.

Attractions After The Drive:
Natural Jungle Paradise Exhibit
Swamp Ride
Birds of Prey Show
Wet Play Area
Reptile Exhibit
Educational Shows
On Saturdays and Sundays, we have an education presentation about the animals at 1 pm in the Safari Theatre. Educational classes for groups, such as a school field trip, can be arranged at our main office.
