Safari Park
Photos | Park Information

Safari Park was created to exhibit animals roaming free in a habitat that is similar to their natural environment. It was opened to the public in 1985 and has undergone several expansions and improvement projects over the years.

We have a variety of animals including tigers, lions, elephants, and giraffes. Successful breeding has maintained our population, which includes some endangered species.

We run a variety of education programs and camps. Be sure to pick up a schedule of events when you visit us at Safari Park.
