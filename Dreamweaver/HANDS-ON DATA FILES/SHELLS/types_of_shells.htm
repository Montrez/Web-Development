There are many different types of shells including these four:

Babylon Shells
Babylon shells are thick with brown blotches found on the outer shell. Most species are found in the tropical Indo-Pacific and live in shallow water.

Cowries
Cowries have a glossy surface and come in a variety of beautiful colors. They are about 200 species of cowries and they are found mainly in the tropics.

Frog Shells
Frog shells take on many shapes. Some have warty lumps which is why they are named frog shells. They are found in the tropical Indo-Pacific on the sand and in coral rubble.

Scallops
Scallops are probably the best known of all seashells. They have a fan-shaped shell and are commonly found in the sand. There are many different species of scallops found all over the world.
