Cleaning Seashells

A seashell that has no animal tissue inside is cleaned by soaking the shell in a solution made up of 50% water and 50% bleach. If there are some barnacles and other material left on the seashell, an instrument such as a dental pick can be used to remove the material.

If you want to add shine to your shells, they can be polished with mineral or baby oil.
