Welcome to our scuba site! We are Hank and Barb, and we live in Florida. We love to scuba dive in the Pompano Beach area of southeast Florida. We were both certified as scuba divers in 1999 and we have each logged over 200 dives since then. Our dive locations page lists many of our favorite reefs and wrecks in this area.

Our favorite scuba activity is taking pictures. Barb likes to photograph invertebrates, like sponges and anemones. The patterns and colors are magnificent. Hank will take a photo of anything that comes within range! The photo gallery showcases some of our best shots.

If you want to dive when you are visiting this area of Florida, drop us a line and we'll be glad to go diving with you!

Let's go diving! --Hank and Barb
