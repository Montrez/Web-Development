Here's four of our best photos. If you have ever tried underwater photography you know how difficult it is to get great photographs! Many factors affect the photos. There are usually particulates floating in the water which show up in the photo. It is important to have a good strobe light, which is a flash used for underwater cameras. A good strobe will help the colors in your photos. Equipment is important!
