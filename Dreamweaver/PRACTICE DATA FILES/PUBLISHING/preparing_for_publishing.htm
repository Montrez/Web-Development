Preparing for Publishing

Before a Web site is published, it should be checked for misspellings and broken hyperlinks. User frustration on the Web is often attributed to hyperlinks that do not work. Broken hyperlinks are links with a destination that is no longer available. Any broken hyperlinks should be corrected before the site is published.You may also want to examine the page contents and remove an element to decrease the weight of the page. Keep in mind users do not wait for pages to load.
