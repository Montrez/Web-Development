AFTER SCHOOL PROGRAM
VOLUNTEERS NEEDED!

Central High School is seeking volunteers to work with children in a after school program. Volunteers will work with groups of children in reading circles, fun and game activities, homework helpers, and listening time. Children of all ages bused to the high shcool to participate in the activites. All volunteers can get course credit for the time spent helping the young students.

Bring your after school fun ideas and suggestions. Snacks are seved and bus pick-up begins at 5:00 p.m. Please join us for some fun and donate your time to this extraordinary program!
